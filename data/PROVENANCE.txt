Dataset description
----------------------------------------------------------------
Per-run measurements of LLM generation stability across a battery
of public benchmark questions, processed through the NeoMundi
runtime measurement pipeline.

Schema (CSV columns):
  provider_id     Provider identifier (e.g. P-001)
  question_id     Benchmark question identifier (e.g. TQ-0001)
  decision        Pipeline decision (ALLOW | FLAG)
  g_score         Stability score in [0, 1] (1 = stable)
  regime          STABLE (>=0.75) | SIGNAL (>=0.50) | CRITICAL (<0.50)
  dg_profile      Temporal drift profile (FLAT | RISING | FALLING | OSC)
  dg_flagged      Drift flag (boolean)
  dg_variation    Token-by-token variation magnitude
  judge_verdict   External judge verdict (CORRECT | INCORRECT)
  is_correct      Boolean projection of judge_verdict
  response_hash   SHA-256 of the response (privacy by design)
  cost_usd        Per-run cost in USD

----------------------------------------------------------------
Method
----------------------------------------------------------------
Stability is measured at runtime over the model's generation
trajectory. Only anonymized JSON logs are produced (UUID,
timestamp, stability_score, SHA-256 hash). No content is stored.

Three FLAG triggers:
  - stability_score < 0.50
  - factual_hallucination_score >= 0.50
  - semantic_risk >= 0.70
